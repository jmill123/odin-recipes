Water

Decription

Everyone needs water to survive. Here is a quick and easy way to get some. Have fun!

  • Ice
  • Love
  1. Obtain Ice from the farthest Speedway
  2. Put in oven safe container
  3. Put in oven for 10 mins at 350 degrees F
  4. Take out of oven (be careful it may be hot)
  5. Add some more ice to cool down if desired
  6. Drink Up!
