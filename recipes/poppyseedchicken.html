Poppy Seed Chicken

Decription

This recipe is super good and it is my favorite. My mom makes this and it is yummy. I recommend this recipe it is good. It has creamy chicken and noodles with cripsy crushed crackers on top.

  • Chicken
  • Noodles of Choice
  • Ritz Crackers
  • Cream of Chicken
  • Poppy Seeds
  • Love
  1. Cook Chicken
  2. Shred Chicken
  3. Crush Ritz Crackers
  4. Put Chicken and Cream of Chicken in pan
  5. Dust with poppy seeds and crushed crackers
  6. Put in oven for 15 mins at 450 degrees F
  7. Eat it
